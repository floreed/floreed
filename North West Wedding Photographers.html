Hi There!

We’re Pete and Laura, wedding photographers based in Lancashire.

 

We love to take beautiful pictures of joyous people as they throw the biggest party of their lives. Natural and fun images are our jam, blending in with guests and capturing special moments (with a bit of artistic flair!).

 

We’ve photographed over 500 weddings all over the world (always up for a destination wedding!), and we feel so lucky to call this our job. When we’re not shooting weddings we’re mostly walking in the forest near our cosy cottage, where we live with our small human Albert and our cat Hugo.

GET IN TOUCH

Real Moments

For us, it's not all about the dress, the shoes, or even all the beautiful decorations.

 

We feel deeply that a wedding is about the people, your people, the party, the atmosphere you've created. Our favourite images are always those meaningful moments between people. Moments you can show to your children which say ‘this is who we were’.

 

Obviously we do photograph the pretty details though - we're not totally immune to their charms.

We love grandparents holding hands and people laughing out loud. We’re excited to grow old together and be those grandparents holding hands.

 

We want to capture moments no one notices and frame them. We want you to be able to look through your wedding pictures and see that your day was even better than you remember.

WEDDING PORTRAITS
PORTRAITS

wedding photographers for people who don't want to pose All Day

We've been photographing people laughing their heads off (at weddings) since 2005. We don't want to pose you for hours, and we don't need to. Your wedding will already be filled of amazing moments just waiting for us to find.

 

We do love a few beautiful portraits of you though. But only for around 15 minutes. We'll find a perfect spot for these portraits and then see what we can create together - the style of these depends on how you are as a couple, we love laughter, we love romance, we'll encourage whatever you give us! And if you don't want any portraits, we won't make you!

Two time winners of best photographers in the north west, and now judges for The Wedding Industry Awards. Our work has taken us all over the world and has been featured in many publications. So, if you want beautiful pictures but don't want a million posey group shots, get in touch.
